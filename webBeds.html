Welcome to WebBeds, the world’s fastest growing provider of accommodation distribution services to the travel industry.

WebBeds is the world’s fastest growing B2B travel intermediary, or ‘bedbank’, providing accommodation and ground product distribution services to the travel industry.

We source content from travel suppliers, connect, aggregate and merchandise that content in our platform, the WebBeds Marketplace, and distribute it to our global network of travel trade clients (our distribution partners), who sell to the travelling public.

Our distribution partners can access the company’s huge inventory of travel products, via market-leading, trade-only booking websites or via simple and seamless API connectivity.

Our supplier partners benefit from our extensive global distribution network of large established online retailers,traditional retail travel agencies, corporate travel managers, supper apps, tourism boards, airlines and wholesalers etc.
